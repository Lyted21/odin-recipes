Recipe for the best lasagna ever

This lasagna will make your mouth water!

Easy and simple to make, try it now! Here are the ingredients:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and flavouring
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

How to make

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe situations
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
